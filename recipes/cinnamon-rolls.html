Cinnamon Rolls

Description

Sweet baked dough filled with a cinnamon-sugar filling.

One of my personal favorite snacks. Can be enjoyed at almost any time.

Ingredients

  • Yeast
  • Flour
  • Sugar
  • Salt
  • Whole Milk
  • Butter
  • Egg
  • Vanilla Extract
  • Cinnamon

Steps

  1. Prepare the dough by mixing and stiring the yeast, milk, sugar, melted butter, and eggs.
  2. Knead and then rise the dough.
  3. Prepare the filling by mixing the softened butter, brown sugar, and cinnamon in a bowl.
  4. Roll out the dough then spread the filling evenly.
  5. Cut and seperate dough.
  6. Preheat at 350 degrees Fahrenheit then bake for 25-30 minutes until they are golden brown.
  7. Make the glazed frosting by mixing powdered sugar, melted butter, vanilla extract, and milk.
  8. Drizzle the frosting all over and enjoy!
