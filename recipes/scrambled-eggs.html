Scrambled Eggs

Description

Classic breakfast dish.

Lots of protein included.

Ingredients

  • 1-2 Eggs

Steps

  1. Turn on the stove and place a pan down.
  2. Crack 1-2 Eggs and place the insides on the stove
  3. Set to high heat and flip the eggs after 1-2 minutes
  4. Wait another 1-2 minutes and take the egg out and serve.
  5. Enjoy!
